Finding dy/dx

\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{dt} \cdot \frac{dt}{dx} = \frac{\frac{dy}{dx}}{\frac{dx}{dt}} \]

\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{2t - 1}{2t + 1} \]
